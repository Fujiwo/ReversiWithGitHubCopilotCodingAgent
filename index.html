How to Play Reversi

  • Click on a valid move (highlighted cells) to place your disc
  • Capture opponent discs by trapping them between your discs
  • The game ends when no valid moves remain
  • The player with the most discs wins

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys - Navigate the board
  • Enter/Space - Place disc
  • Ctrl+U - Undo move
  • Ctrl+R - Restart game
